Tournament Rules & Guidelines

1. Player Eligibility

  • Must be 13 years or older
  • Valid in-game account required
  • No account sharing allowed
  • One account per person

2. Fair Play Policy

  • No hacking or cheating allowed
  • No account boosting or trading
  • No abusive language or behavior
  • Violation results in permanent ban

3. Match Rules

  • Follow custom room settings strictly
  • Don't disconnect intentionally
  • Respect all referees' decisions
  • Report bugs immediately

4. Prize Distribution

  • Winners announced within 24 hours
  • Prizes credited to registered account
  • Tax applicable as per law
  • Disputes resolved by admin team

5. Code of Conduct

  • Respect all participants
  • No cheating or hacking
  • Play with sportsmanship
  • Follow platform guidelines

6. Anti-Cheat System

  • 24/7 monitoring of all matches
  • Advanced AI detection system
  • Automatic flagging of suspicious activity
  • Manual review by trained staff
